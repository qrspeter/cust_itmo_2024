Info¶

The noise of optical detectors (including CCDs) depends on temperature. Usually noise increases with increasing temperature, so receivers are often cooled. The CCD in the example is usually cooled down to -70C. The matrix itself has a size of 1024$\times$128 elements, but averaging "by columns" gives an one-dimensional array of 1024$\times$1.

This is a background noise measurement, not a spectral one, and we do not need to use wavelength. Use the numbers of the matrix elements only (corresponding to the line number in the file).
